Understanding coffee terminology makes you a better barista. These guides cover the essential terms: extraction, TDS, bloom, crema, ristretto, lungo, micro-foam, and more. Whether you're reading a recipe and hit an unfamiliar term, or you want to deepen your coffee knowledge, this collection covers the full vocabulary of espresso and specialty coffee.
