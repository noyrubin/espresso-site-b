Barista At Home is a resource for people who want to brew great espresso at home without wading through marketing fluff or affiliate-driven recommendations.

## What We Cover

- **Guides** — Step-by-step tutorials on espresso technique, from [pulling your first shot](/guides/getting-started/) to advanced dialing-in and [troubleshooting](/guides/espresso-troubleshooting/).
- **Equipment** — Honest recommendations for [espresso machines](/guides/best-espresso-machines-beginners/) and [grinders](/guides/best-espresso-grinders/) at every price point, based on real-world usability rather than spec sheets.
- **Recipes** — Classic espresso drink recipes ([americanos](/recipes/americano/), [cortados](/recipes/cortado/), [flat whites](/recipes/flat-white/)) with exact ratios and technique tips.

## Our Editorial Approach

Every recommendation on this site is based on hands-on experience with the equipment and techniques we describe. We do not recommend products we have not used, and we do not rank equipment based on affiliate commission rates.

When we cite data, we link to the source. When we share opinions, we label them as such. When something is outside our expertise, we say so.
